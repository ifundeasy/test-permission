---
description: Break work down and produce an effort estimate with assumptions and risk ranges
argument-hint: <task or feature>
---
Estimate `$ARGUMENTS`.

1. Decompose into concrete tasks (implementation, tests, migration, review, rollout).
2. Give a range per task (optimistic / likely / pessimistic) with the assumptions behind it and the
   unknowns that would move it.
3. Note dependencies, sequencing, and what could be parallelized.
4. Summarize a total range and the top uncertainties that should be resolved first. Estimation
   only — do not start the work.
